About Me

After graduating from HKUST with a physics degree, I worked as a channel sales in an IT distributor for 2 years. I also taught swimming part-time. Though I had great satisfaction building relationships with my clients and partners, I do not feel that is a career path to persuit further. I started to search for the next thing that exites me.

Then I came across some programming youtube tutorials and decided to give it a try. I started learning computer science basic concepts like loops, functions and data structures with Python. Doing Codewar kata's gradually became one of my past-times. Programming rekindled my passion for solving complex problems. After about 3 months from my first piece of code, here I am.

From here on now, I hope to struggle with bugs as much as I need to. Surely this will be a fruitful journey.
